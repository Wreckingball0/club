What We Do




In Video Production Club, we make vidoes. Some of the projects that we produce are documentaries, short films, tutorials, and much more. Not only do we make films, but we produce them. We write our own scripts, with everyones ideas all pitched in, making sure the film isnt only great, but that it makes everyone happy, from the audiance to us behind the scenes. We also learn how to hold and work a camera, because without a camera, the films is only a play. And after everything is recorded onto the camera, we edit everything into the final product. We learn all of this plus more in Video Production Club.
